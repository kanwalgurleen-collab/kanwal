About Me

AI Educator, Researcher, Academic Leader, and a dedicated academician, researcher, and administrator with over 18 years of experience in Management and Business Studies, serving as Head of Department at Apeejay Institute of Management & Engineering, Jalandhar. Throughout my academic journey, I have maintained a strong record of scholastic excellence and professional commitment. I believe in integrity, loyalty, and long-term organizational contribution, which is reflected in my extensive tenure across institutions, including more than a decade of continuous service at my current organization.

Over the years, I have successfully combined teaching, research, academic leadership, and institutional development. Alongside delivering high-quality instruction to MBA and BBA students, I have actively contributed to various strategic and administrative functions that support organizational growth and academic excellence.

Currently, I serve at Apeejay Institute of Management & Engineering Technical Campus, Punjab, India, holding multiple leadership and administrative responsibilities, including Head – Training & Placements, Head – Social Media, Co-Coordinator – Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC), Coordinator – Counseling & Admissions, and Key Member of Academic Planning, Perspective Planning, and Strategic Development Committees.

My teaching expertise spans a wide range of management disciplines, including Business Management, Marketing Management, Digital Marketing, Social Media Marketing, Business Analytics, Marketing Analytics, Advertising Management, Strategic Management, and Statistical Techniques. I am passionate about integrating contemporary industry practices and emerging technologies into classroom learning to enhance student engagement and employability.

As an active researcher, I have published more than 50 research papers in reputed national and international journals, presented research work at numerous conferences, and contributed extensively to academic development through faculty development programs, seminars, workshops, and expert lectures. My commitment to continuous learning is reflected in multiple professional certifications, including Google Certified Educator credentials and Six Sigma Green Belt certification.

Beyond academics, I possess strong expertise in student counseling, admissions, placements, institutional planning, quality assurance, content development, statistical analysis, digital communication, social media strategy, and academic administration. My professional objective is to continue contributing to educational excellence, research advancement, student development, and institutional growth through innovation, leadership, and lifelong learning.
